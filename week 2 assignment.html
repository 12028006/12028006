IMPORTANT FIELD

STUDENT
" A student is primarily a person enrolled in a school or other educational institution and who is under learning with goals of acquiring knowledge, developing professions and achieving easy employment at a particular field"

TEACHER
A teacher is a person who helps people to learn. A teacher often works in a classroom. There are many different kinds of teachers. ... Teachers often explain new knowledge, write on a blackboard or whiteboard, sit behind their desks on chairs.

FARMER
A farmer (also called an agriculturer) is a person engaged in agriculture, raising living organisms for food or raw materials. The term usually applies to people who do some combination of raising field crops, orchards, vineyards, poultry, or other livestock.
